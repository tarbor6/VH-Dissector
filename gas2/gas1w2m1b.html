Notice that the veli muscles encase the nasopharyngeal space.

What is the function of these muscles?

Tensor veli palatini = tenses soft palate and opens the pharyngotympanic tube via CN V3

Levator veli palatini = elevates the soft palate above the neutral position via CN X
