Anterior triangle is composed of 4 triangles, defined in the following way:

  • Gold - Submental triangle = hyoid bone, anterior bewlly of the diagastric, midline
  • White - Submandibular triangle = mandible, anterior and posterior bellies of the digastric
  • Red - Muscular triangle = sternocleidomastoid, omohyoid, midline
  • Blue - Carotid Triangle = posterior belly of the digastric, sternocleidomastoid, omohyoid

The posterior triangle is formed by one triangle, defined in the following way:

  • Green - Posterior Triangle = Sternocleidomastoid, clavicle,trapezius
