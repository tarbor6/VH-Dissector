Identify the esophageal hiatus.

At what thoracic level is this located?

T 10 (T X).

What anatomical components pass through this opening?

Esophagus, anterior and posterior vagal trunks, esophageal branches of left gastric artery and vein.
Identify the aortic hiatus.

At what thoracic level is this opening located?

T 12 (T XII).

What anatomical components pass through this opening?

Aorta, thoracic duct, azygos vein.
