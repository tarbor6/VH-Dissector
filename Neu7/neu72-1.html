Begin with the right side. of the cadaver's head.

The middle ear contains the components that convert vibration to nervous system impulses.

What are the names of these components?

Tympanic membrane, malleus, incus, and stapes.

The stapes attaches to the oval window, which transmits vibration to the cochlear system.

Atmospheric equilibrium is maintained via the pharyngotympanic tube which connects the inner ear to the pharynx.
