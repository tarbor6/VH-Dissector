The erectile tissues are covered by the following muscles:

Add the ischiocavernosus muscles which cover the corpus cavernosi. The corpus cavernosi joins at the midline to form the glans clitoris.
Add the bulbospongiosus muscles which cover and surround the remaining components of the vestibule.

Note that the urethral orifice is just posterior to the glans clitoris. The glas clitoris will serve as a landmark when doing a urinary catheterization of a female.

Add the external anal sphincter.

What are the three muscular components of the external anal sphincter?

Deep: closes the anal canal.

Superficial: anchors to the perineal body.

Subcutaneous: Surrounds the aperture.

Identify them in the MPR window.
