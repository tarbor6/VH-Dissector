Add the ovaries.
Add the ovarian arteries. Note that like the testicular arteries, they arise frome the abdominal aorta.
Add the ovarian veins. Note that like the testicular veins the right ovarian vein drains into the inferior vena cava and the left ovarian vein drains into the left renal vein.
Add the ligaments of the ovaries..
Add the suspensory (infundibulopelvic) ligament of the ovary.

These ligaments are extra peritoneal folds that contain the ovarian arteries, veins and lymphatics. The formation of these is better explained embryologically. Please review the embryology of the evolution of the female genital ducts. (Page 266 of Langman's Medical Embryology)
