Start with the right lung.

With each lobe highlighted, rotate the lung to identify the fissures that separate the lobes and the orientation of these lobes to the major stuctures of the mediastinum.
Add the superior lobe of right lung. The apex of the superior lobe of the right lung lies above the first rib. Demonstrate this by adding the first rib to the 3D cadaver.
Add the middle lobe of right lung.
Add the inferior lobe of right lung.

Knowing the locations of the superior, middle and inferior lobes of the right lung, Discuss where you would place your stethoscope to hear breath sounds from each lobe.
