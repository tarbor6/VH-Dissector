CAR PART 3 Module 1

OBJECTIVES:

2.2.1   Identify the parietal and visceral pleura, their different components, and the pleural recesses.

2.2.2   Indicate the surface projections of the different components of the parietal and visceral pleura.

2.2.3   Identify the lobes of the right and left lungs.

2.2.4   Identify impressions made on the surfaces of each lung by surrounding structures.

2.2.5   Distinguish the surface projections of the fissures and lobes of the lungs, the heart, pericardium and the great vessels.

READINGS:

Gray's Anatomy for Students (Fourth Edition): Pages: 125-247. See Green Highlighted sections for clinical correlation.
