What are the layers of the protective coatings of the spinal cord?

Dura mater

Arachnoid mater

Pia mater

Note how the protective layers change, with respect to the spinal cord, spinal nerves,spinal ganglia, at varying levels of the spinal cord.

Does the spinal dura mater cover the spinal ganglion?

Yes, the spinal dura mater covers the nerve bundle until it becomes the spinal nerve. This covering is called the dorsal root sheath or the dural sleeve.
