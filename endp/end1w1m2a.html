Pelvis and Perineum: Module 2 - Nerves, Vessels and Lymphatic Drainage

OBJECTIVES:

END1.1.7   Know the origins of the nerves supplying the pelvis and perineum.

END1.1.8   Trace the course of the pudendal nerve and its branches to all structures in the perineum.

END1.1.9   Follow the flow of blood into and out of the structures of the pelvis and perineum.

END1.1.10   Identify the lymphatic drainage of structures in the pelvis and perineum.

END1.1.11   Identify the sites of aggregations of lymph nodes receiving lympahtic drainage from various areas of the pelvis and perineum (whether or not they are seen on the images).

READINGS:

Gray's Anatomy for Students (Fourth Edition): Pages: 480-524. See Green Highlighted sections for clinical correlation.
