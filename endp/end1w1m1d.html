Draw an imaginary line between the ischial tuberosities. This separates the posterior muscular pelvic diaphragm, which controls defecation, from the urogenital triangle which contains the vaginal and the urethral openings.

What are the landmarks that delineate the urogenital triangle?

The apex of the triangle is the pubic symphysis.

The base of the triangle is the imaginary line connecting the two ischial tuberosities.
