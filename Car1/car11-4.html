Add the sternum and remove the left side of the thorax. This will allow us to visualize the compartments of the mediastinum.
Using the Highlight and Draw tools, identify thoracic vertebrae T-IV and T-V. Then draw a line between the sternal angle and the space between T-IV and T-V. Superior to this line is the superior mediastinum.
Using the Highlight and Draw tools, identify the anterior, middle and posterior compartments of the inferior mediastinum.

What is contained in the middle compartment of the mediastinum?

The pericardium which contains the heart.

Through which mediastinal compartments does the esophagus pass?

The superior and posterior mediastinal compartments.
