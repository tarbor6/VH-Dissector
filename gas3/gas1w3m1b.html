Besides taste, temperature sensation and making funny faces at your friends, what is the function of the tongue?

Moves food around in the oral cavity and along with mastication prepares a bolus of food to be swallowed.

What nerve innervates the muscular control of the tongue?

Hypoglossal CN XII.
