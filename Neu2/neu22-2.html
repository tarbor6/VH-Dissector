Why is it safest to perform a lumbar puncture between L2 and L3 or L3 and L4?

Because the spinal cord ends at L1, and a needle placed in the spinal column will separate - rather than puncture - the nerves.

What is the function of the filum terminale?

It anchors the spinal cord in the spinal column.
