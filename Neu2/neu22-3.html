The name of the denticulate ligaments orginates from the latin "dentatus". Why?

Because they look like teeth.

From what protective covering do the dentate ligaments originate?

Pia mater.

What role do the dentate ligaments play for the spinal cord?

They stabilize the spinal cord in the spinal column and prevent the cord from twisting.
