Focus on the right orbit. Using the Highlight tool, identify the following bones. Notice how the bones light up in Cross-Sections panels:

The palatine bone exists in this cadaver. How would you find it?

From the VHDissector toolbar:

  • Tap on the Anatomy icon.
  • Tap on Index (left side of the screen).
  • Type the word palatine in the search box.
  • Tap on the "starburst" beside the name of a structure to see it highlighted on the cadaver.

Note that you may need to rotate the cadaver to see the highlighted area.
