What artery supplies most of the organs (#8 to #12) listed above?

The superior messenteric artery.

Where is the transition zone of the organs supplied by the superior and inferior messenteric arteries?

The mid transverse colon. The transverse colon is considered a watershed area because there is no exact transition area for every person.

Why would it be important to understand the arterial supply of the transverse colon?

During colonic resection, the blood supply to the preserved colon must be defined and ensured.
