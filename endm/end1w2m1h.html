What does the parasympathetic nervous system control in the male genitalia control?

Vasodilitation

Stimulate erection

In other words: The Parasympatheic nervous system Points.

What does the sympathetic nervous system contron in the the male genitalia control?

Smooth muscle contration of the internal urethral sphincter in men

Smooth muscle contraction of the accessory glands of the reproductive system

Helps to move secretions from the epididymis and associated glands into the urethra for ejaculation.

In other words, the Sympathetic nervous system Shoots.
